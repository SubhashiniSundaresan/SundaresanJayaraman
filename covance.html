Project: Covance Odyssey Pega Migration (Onsite)


Project: Covance Odyssey Pega Migration (Onsite)
Designation at Virtusa: QA Manager
Role in the project: QA Manager
Duration – May 2015 to till date
Team Size – 144
Business Problem: Covance Inc., Drug Development Service Company’s Odyssey application, is a CRM app catering to a pharmaceutical client for 11 Services requests like Insurance Verification, Authorization Assistance, and Information services, Product Assistance, Financial Assistance and Claims. Virtusa engagement is to convert and upgrade Odyssey application suite as a platform and a core product for Covance multiple clients with enhanced functionalities and features with Pega 6.3 to Pega 7.8.1 upgrade. New requirements /functional enhancements are to be gathered, analyzed, developed and tested for quality and consistency. Integration and testing, Test Automation using Virtusa Test Automation Framework (VTAF) using Selenium and Performance Testing of the migrated application suite, CTI Performance Testing and addition of 18 Covance Clients to the Core Product are in scope. eService application, enabling Insurance Verification and Authorization Assistance is developed for a client to be integrated Catalyst is also to be tested.
Environment: Pega 6.3, Pega 7.8.1, Oracle, HP Performance Center 12.01 /Load Runner Virtual User Generator 12.01, Virtusa Test Automation Framework(VTAF 3.0) using Selenium, Chorus Project Management Tool ,JIRA, BrightCloud’s CTI Agent Emulator, Calls Emulator, CTIOS Agent Desktop and App Dynamics. MS Visio
Project Role:
  • Project Management of Odyssey Project comprising of manual, automation and performance testing, business analysis and testability of requirements
  • Participate and contribute in Business Elaboration sessions for the Service Requests with business stakeholders and gather high level requirements
  • Analyze and review the high level requirements to ensure that they meet the stated and implied needs of the customer and other business stakeholders
  • Support capturing the requirements using Business Requirement Specification document which forms the basis of planning and development
  • Analyze and review Business Requirements Specifications, Business Rules, UI Mock Ups and Property Matrix documents
  • Review the feedbacks from Business on the BRS documentation and update the same
  • Prepare QA Q & A documentation on requirements, business rules and get clarifications
  • Gather non-functional Requirements (Performance, Security and Browser Testing) from Business, Business Analysts, Architects and validate
  • Preparation and review of QA Test Approach, Test Automation Approach and Performance Test Approach
  • Prepare the Test Strategy based on the types of testing techniques including the functional and non-functional requirements (Performance Testing -Load, Stress and Volume Tests)
  • Estimate the size of automation test suite development and performance testing
  • Support the leads in preparing Automation and Performance Test Plans
  • Manage and support Performance Testing and Functional Test Automation Testing tasks
  • Design, Lead and Review Manual, Functional Automation Architecture, Test Suite, Performance Testing Architecture and Test Plans
  • Lead, review and identify of test scenarios for automation and performance testing and define Performance SLAs with business
  • Schedule and conduct walkthrough to stake holders (Business, Development and UAT) after internal reviews/feedbacks incorporated for the selection of automation and performance test scenarios and get sign off
  • Overseeing the setup the Automation and Performance test environment and resource planning
  • Plan and execute Pega CTI Call Popup Performance Testing with CTIOS and BrightCloud
  • Manage and support design of test scripts in VTAF 3.0 and HP Performance Center tool
  • Manage development of Test data for Performance Testing-Virtual User Matrix for Load Testing-Guide the team for increasing the static and transactional data volume to 3.6 million records in Performance Testing Environment
  • Co-ordinate with Offshore on daily basis and support offshore for clarifications/issues and provide detailed info and for development of automation test scripts and performance test scripts
  • Scripting guidelines for virtual user generation scripting/review of LoadRunner Scripts/Controller Scenarios
  • Oversee the Lead Review on Quality Assurance documents and Test Scripts prepared by offshore and provide review comments.
  • Review, standardization and upgrade for Selenium Automation Scripts
  • Manage execution of functional automation suites, share test results, identify the defects and through defects triage meeting resolve the defects
  • Manage execution of Performance Test Suite, share test results, identify the performance issues ,collection and analysis of Load Testing results for Load testing runs
  • Daily triage meetings and track after discussion with development and architectural leads
  • Support UAT testing by Business and conduct defect triage meetings
  • Track, escalate risks which might adversely affect the success and work with appropriate resources for resolution
  • Responsible for project monitoring and tracking, execution of test automation for each release, identifying dependencies, risks and mitigation
  • Manage team for daily status reports, weekly status reports on QA and automation
  • Manage Performance Results Reporting-Client and Server Side Metrics and Reporting on daily basis and conduct Weekly Status Report Meeting with Development, Database and Infrastructure Managers and IT Director of Covance
  • Plan and conduct Weekly Management Meetings for QA and Performance Testing Status with Virtusa and Covance IT stakeholders and report to Senior Management
